Why BadgeIt?

How to BadgeIt?

Step 1: Create your badge template.

How to BadgeIt?

Step 2: In the Start page, enter your project name, add your template and the CSV input file. Choose the fields you would like on your badge from the multiple select box that appears. Enter the badge resolution details and submit.

How to BadgeIt?

Step 3: Optionally, you can add a QR code to your badges by checking the QR code box in the Start page. Choose the fields you would like encoded in your QR code and proceed.

How to BadgeIt?

Step 4: In the Create page that appears, customise your badges by using the toolbox, and scaling or moving the objects on the canvas. Click next to create all badges.

How to BadgeIt?

Step 5: The badges are created and the toolbox populates with options to download the images as a zip file or view a tiled HTML page. You may go back by clicking the Back button to make any changes and re-create the badges.

How to BadgeIt?

Step 6: The Download zip button appears on clicking Zip files, and the file is downloaded with the Project name you entered.

How to BadgeIt?

Step 7: All your badges are displayed in a tiled version on clicking View all in Sheet. You can optionally print to PDF from your browser.

How to BadgeIt?

Step 8: Click Finish when you are done creating the badges.
