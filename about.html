About

We love events. We organize them, attend them, have fun in them, meet some of the best people in them. Tech conferences, hackathons, TEDx events, Barcamps, unconferences, and what not.

BadgeIt, is our first attempt at giving back to the awesome events that we've gained so much from. We hope you'd love it.
